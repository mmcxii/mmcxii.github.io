About Me

Hello! My name is Nich Secord, I am a front-end leaning full stack developer (in training until November 2019) based in the Seattle, Washigton area. What I enjoy most about this industry is creating beautiful and responsive layouts that look great on any screen size small or large. I am a proponent of using semantic HTML in tandem with CSS Grid and Flexbox as opposed to "div-soup" to create accesible sites for those that are incapable of viewing the page as well. I am firm in this belief because as stewards of the front-end it is fundamental to our job to ensure that the internet be easy to use for everyone, regardless of ability.

When away from the office I love spending time outdoors and/ or with friends. I enjoy skiing, hiking, and camping; or for a more leisurly time I love a great variety of beer, wine, and spirits. For my money there's no better cocktail than a bourbon Manhattan with a splash of Grand Marnier!
